Set initial population seeding range. min® / sqrt(3) is a conservative setting, ensuring that the cubic diagonal of initial population range will fit in the lowest coverage radius of all nodes.

Genetic algorithm options

Vectorized specifies if the the GA is to be run with multiple individuals passed to it for evaluation at once or not.
